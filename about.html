About Us

Edprime Consultants was founded in the year 2020. We are one of the best and most comprehensive study-abroad consultants in India. Our services include counselling students based on career options and matching them to the best Colleges/Universities in various countries like Canada, Australia, the UK and Germany based on their profile, choice, background, and aspirations for suitable careers.

Why We Started Edprime Consultants ?

Having personal years of experience from studying, traveling and working abroad, we understand how difficult the process of getting reliable information about study abroad program can be. Most people are misguided by the information they obtained from their friends, relatives and even from some online sources. For a study abroad program you need trusted and correct information.

We are different because we have already passed through the process and understood what it takes to study abroad. We advise you based on our experience and we match you to the best university & course according to your profile and choices. Basically, it is our work to help you solve all the problems that are associated with studying overseas.

Why Are We Different?

We give you first-hand information that is reliable and authentic. We are honest and provide you everything you need to know about study abroad programs in various countries.

We care a lot about what we do and provide unbiased and expert counselling to students.
